About ChemSci-2023

CHEMSCI2023 : LEADERS IN THE FIELD SYMPOSIUM is being held in India for young investigators in the field of chemistry. This will be the fourth edition of the RSC sponsored virtual symposium and is organized by The Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR) Bangalore in association with the Chemical Science journal from January 23 to 25, 2023.

Under the banner of our flagship open access journal, Chemical Science, we’ll be shining a spotlight on cutting-edge chemistry research and giving it the sort of attention that drives scientific progress and makes a difference. We warmly invite you to join us for the series of journal symposia, and look forward to welcoming you at this online event.

This in-person meeting at JNCASR Bangalore aims at providing a forum to the rapidly growing chemistry community in India especially to the young and early career researchers.

Poster Session


How to submit your Poster:

Researchers will have the opportunity to participate and present their best research at the poster session and stand a chance to win exciting Best Poster Prizes from Chemical Science journal. 

Research Scholars interested to present their posters are requested to submit their poster abstracts through the Google Form before 07th Jan 2023. You will be intimated if your poster is selected for the competition by 10th Jan 2023. You may arrange for your travel to attend the event only after you receive confirmation of your poster selection.  

Four selected Best Poster Awardees will also have the opportunity to present their work orally on the last day of the conference.

For further details of the poster competition and to submit your poster abstract(via the Google Form).

Instructions for selected Posters:

Poster session will happen on Monday, 23rd January 2023 and Tuesday, 24th January 2023 starting from 14.00 to 15.30 IST at JNCASR Campus. Winners from the poster session will be selected by Tuesday, 24th January 2023. The winners name with their poster number will be announced during the session of the symposium. The top 4 poster prize winners will be given the opportunity to present their research as Flash presentation on the last day of this symposium.

All poster participates are requested to make your PowerPoint presentation ready along with your poster before you reach the campus, so that you get enough time to prepare your talk if you get selected for the Flash talk.

Poster Guidelines:

1. Please note the following details for the poster dimensions The poster should be in the portrait format, with the following dimensions: Poster Size- 3 ft. (width) x 4 ft. (height) or 36 inches (width) x 48 inches (height) Format: Portrait

Any other format such as landscape will not be accepted for display.

Venue of poster session - AMRL Foyer & SSL Foyer

2. Please be mindful of appropriate permissions while presenting copyright images.

3. Please be mindful of prospective journal policies before presenting unpublished data. Responsibility for the data rests on the presenter.

Judging Criteria

1. Research Originality

2. Potential Impact

3. Comprehensiveness

4. Informative

5. Design

6. Presentation Skill

Flash Presentation Guideline

Oral Flash Presentation guidelines of the winning posters will be informed in details by email on 24th January, 2023.

FAQ

1. What if my poster size does not match with the recommended size? Will my poster be disqualified?
Yes. Stick to the recommended size. It will better fit with our poster display stand/curtain.

2. What are the guidelines for poster winners giving talks on the final day?
If chosen as a poster winner to present on the final day, you will be provided with information before 24th January 2023.

3. What if, I want to present a poster, but I forgot to include this information on the registration form?
Please re-register on the website before the deadline.
